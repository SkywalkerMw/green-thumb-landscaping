Our Story

Cultivating beauty,
one garden at a time

Est. 2015 — Lilongwe

A family passion
turned profession

Founded in 2015, Green Thumb began as a small family business helping neighbours with basic lawn care. Today, we are one of Lilongwe's most trusted landscaping firms — serving private residences, offices, schools, and estates.

Our mission is simple: create outdoor spaces people love to live in — using plants that thrive in Malawi's climate, water-wise designs, and honest workmanship that stands the test of time.
