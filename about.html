About Green Thumb Landscaping

We're a passionate team of landscapers based in Lilongwe, dedicated to turning ordinary outdoor spaces into beautiful, functional, and sustainable environments. With over 10 years of experience, we bring creativity, reliability, and eco-friendly practices to every project.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Green Thumb started as a small family business helping neighbors with basic lawn care. Today, we serve homes, offices, schools, and estates across Lilongwe with full landscaping design, installation, and maintenance services.

Our mission is simple: Create outdoor spaces people love to live in — using plants that thrive in Malawi's climate, water-wise designs, and honest workmanship.
